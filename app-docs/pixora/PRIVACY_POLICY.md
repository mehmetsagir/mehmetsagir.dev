# Privacy Policy - Pixora AI Image Editor

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Device Information

- Device ID (anonymized)
- Device model and operating system
- App version and usage statistics
- Crash reports and error logs

### 1.2 User Content

- Images you upload for processing
- Text prompts you provide
- Chat history and conversations
- Feedback and support requests

### 1.3 Usage Data

- App usage patterns
- Feature usage statistics
- Performance metrics
- Error logs and debugging information

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Core Functionality

- Process your images using AI models
- Store your chat history locally on your device
- Provide customer support
- Improve app performance and features

### 2.2 Analytics and Improvement

- Analyze app usage to improve user experience
- Monitor app performance and stability
- Identify and fix bugs and issues
- Develop new features based on usage patterns

### 2.3 Communication

- Send notifications about completed operations
- Provide important app updates
- Respond to your feedback and support requests

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage

- Your images and chat history are stored locally on your device
- Images are converted to Base64 format for secure local storage
- No personal data is stored on our servers permanently

### 3.2 Data Processing

- Images are processed by third-party AI services (Fall AI)
- Images are not permanently stored on our servers
- Processing is done securely and temporarily

### 3.3 Security Measures

- All data transmission is encrypted
- Local storage is protected by device security
- Regular security audits and updates
- No unauthorized access to your data

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 AI Processing Services

- **Fall AI**: Used for image processing and generation
- **Supabase**: Used for analytics and error logging
- **Apple/Google**: Used for app store services and payments

### 4.2 Data Sharing

- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not share your images with third parties
- Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
- Only necessary data is shared with service providers

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Data Control

- You can delete your chat history at any time
- You can disable notifications in app settings
- You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app
- You can request data deletion by contacting us

### 5.2 Privacy Settings

- Control notification permissions
- Manage data sharing preferences
- Choose which features to use
- Opt out of analytics (if available)

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Local Data

- Chat history: Stored locally until you delete it
- Images: Stored locally until you delete them
- Settings: Stored locally until you change them

### 6.2 Server Data

- Analytics data: Retained for 2 years
- Error logs: Retained for 1 year
- Support requests: Retained for 3 years
- Payment data: Retained as required by law

## 7. Children's Privacy

- Our app is not intended for children under 13
- We do not knowingly collect data from children
- If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately
- We will delete any data from children upon request

## 8. International Users

- Data is processed in accordance with local laws
- EU users have additional rights under GDPR
- California users have additional rights under CCPA
- We comply with applicable privacy regulations

## 9. Changes to This Policy

- We may update this policy from time to time
- Changes will be posted in the app and on our website
- Significant changes will be communicated via app notification
- Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Website**: https://mehmetsagir.dev
- **App**: Use the "Send Feedback" feature in settings
